About Us

Pathshala (A Brand of Brainchild Ventures LLP) is India's latest upcoming online marketplace connecting students from everywhere of every course to the teachers around the country on just a click & catering other educational needs. Students of classes K-12 can register and look for trusted tuition centres, verified home tutors, highly rated online tutors, can find answers to NCERT questions or other questions, hand-written notes on various topics and subjects, and so on. It caters the students to connect with the teachers or professionals involved into imparting their expertise. This platform bridges the gap between the two and allow exploring the new ideas and eventually learn by the subject experts.

Further, students preparing for entrances like IIT-JEE, NEET, AIIMS, UPSC, Different College Entrances can aim bigger with easy access to all study material, classes, tutor listings, etc. Pathshala gives equal importance to vocational courses like Content Writing, Animations, Gym/Physical Education, etc which will add to your skill set and will give you an edge in this competitive real world or you could just enjoy yourself with our teachers from the field for dance, theatre, art, culture, music etc. for short time courses/classes/workshops like Bollywood dance classes, cooking classes, art classes near you. The best way to choose from our list of teachers is on the basis of ratings on a set of parameters and reviews given by fellow students which will help students to shortlist the best as per your needs. Ratings are the unique selling point of Pathshala which enables students to opt for best and promotes transparency.

Further, Pathshala is a place where you can kick start your career as a teacher or give your existing tuitions classes and coaching centres a competitive edge and Pathshala will facilitate all your business needs parallelly by listing you on our website for FREE. We invite every individual to be a part of Pathshala family, be it a homemaker, freelancer, part-timers, professionals, hobby experts, vocational course coaches, etc. because we aim at opening teaching opportunities through our portal in every field.
