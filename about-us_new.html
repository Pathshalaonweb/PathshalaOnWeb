About Us

Pathshala (Brainchild Ventures LLP) is an India's latest upcoming online platform to connect students from class K-12 to students wanting to inculcate different hobbies with the help of most trusted local tutors. It caters the students to connect with the teachers or professionals involved into imparting their expertise. This platform bridges the gap between the two and allow exploring the new ideas and eventually learn by the subject matter expert.

Pathshala (Brainchild Ventures LLP) is a place where you can kick start your career as a teacher or at the same time enhance your existing tuitions and coaching centers. We invite every individual to be a part of Pathshala family, be it a homemaker, freelancer, part-timers, professionals, hobby experts etc.

The best way to choose from our list of teachers is based on ratings given by the fellow students which helps you to shortlist the best for yourself. Ratings are the unique aspect of Pathshala which enables you to opt for best and promotes transparency. Each teacher is rated based on set parameters and is openly available for further scrutiny.
